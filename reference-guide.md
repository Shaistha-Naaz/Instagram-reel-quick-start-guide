## User Support and Best Practices
This section provides additional guidance for managing your Reels after publishing. It includes best practices, accessibility recommendations, troubleshooting information, and official resources to help you create high-quality content and resolve common issues.

### Tips for Better Reels

- Record videos in portrait orientation.
- Use good lighting.
- Keep videos short and engaging.
- Add captions for accessibility.
- Choose a high-quality cover image.
- Use trending music when appropriate.
- Preview the Reel before publishing.

### Managing Your Reel After Posting
After publishing your Reel, Instagram provides several options to help you manage and monitor its performance. The following actions can be used to update, share, or control your Reel after it has been posted.

After your Reel is live, you can:

- Share it to your Story for a second wave of visibility
- View likes, comments, and reshares
- Control who can remix your Reel or mention you in captions
- Edit comment filters
- Delete the Reel if needed

### Accessibility Recommendations
Creating accessible content helps make your Reels easier to understand and enjoy for a wider audience. Consider the following recommendations to improve the accessibility of your videos.

To improve accessibility:

- Add captions.
- Use readable text sizes.
- Ensure sufficient color contrast.
- Avoid excessive flashing effects.
- Speak clearly in voice recordings.

### Troubleshooting Common Issues

| Problem | Likely cause | Fix |
|----------|----------| ---------- |
| Upload failed | Poor internet connection | Check network and retry |
| Video looks blurry | Video quality is low | Enable high-quality uploads and use recommended video specifications |
| Audio unavailable | Regional licensing restrictions | Select another audio track or use original audio |
| Video keeps becoming a Reel instead of a feed post | Instagram auto-routes vertical video into Reels | Choose "Post" explicitly instead of "Reel" when creating |
| Reel is cropped on the profile grid | Grid preview crop differs from full-screen crop | Keep important parts of the video near the center |
